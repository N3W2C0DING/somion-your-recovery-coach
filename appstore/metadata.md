## Description (4000 chars max)

Somion is a recovery-aware workout coach that uses your Oura Ring data to recommend the right training each day — based on sleep, readiness, HRV, and how you actually feel.

**THE PROBLEM**
Most training apps ignore how your body is recovering. They give you the same program whether you slept 4 hours or 8. The result? Overtraining, injuries, burnout, or guilt on rest days.

**THE SOLUTION**
Somion reads your Oura Ring data — sleep score, readiness, HRV, resting heart rate, temperature — and combines it with a quick morning check-in (soreness, energy, motivation) to recommend the right intensity, exercises, and duration for today.

**HOW IT WORKS**
1. Connect your Oura Ring
2. Complete a 30-second morning check-in
3. Get a personalized workout recommendation with exercises, sets, reps, and rest times
4. Train with confidence that today's session matches your recovery

**KEY FEATURES**
• Recovery-aware daily recommendations — push, maintain, or recover
• AI-generated workout sessions tailored to your readiness
• Morning check-in that adjusts your plan based on how you feel
• 30-day recovery trends with HRV, sleep, and readiness charts
• Training history with consistency tracking
• Customizable coaching tone and training preferences
• Beautiful dark-mode interface designed for calm focus

**WHAT YOU NEED**
• An Oura Ring (Gen 2 or Gen 3)
• An Oura account with a Personal Access Token

**IMPORTANT**
Somion is a fitness tool, not a medical device. Recommendations are for general fitness guidance only. Always consult a healthcare professional before starting any exercise program.

## Review Notes (for Apple reviewers)
Somion is a fitness coaching app that integrates with the Oura Ring API to provide recovery-aware workout recommendations. The app requires an Oura Ring and a Personal Access Token to function with real data. Without an Oura connection, the app displays sample data for demonstration purposes.

To test:
1. Create an account using email and password
2. The app will show sample data by default
3. Navigate to Settings to connect an Oura Ring (requires a real Oura account)
4. The Today page shows daily recommendations; the Train page shows the full workout

No special demo account is needed — the app works with sample data when Oura is not connected.
